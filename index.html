Rules
Same
Capture cards that have the same ranks on 2 or more sides.
Wall
(Same rule only) Treats the outside walls as 'A' ranks.
Plus
Capture cards that add up to the same sum on 2 adjacent sides.
Combo
Cards captured with Same, Plus or Advance will chain more cards based on higher ranks.
Open
All cards face up.
Elemental
Imbues certain cards and spaces with elements that grant +1 if matched or -1 if not.
House Rules
Advance
Move into an opposing card's spot after capture.
Memory
Card ranks fade away soon after being placed on the board.
Swap
Once per game, swap a placed card out for a different card.
Slide
Use your turn to move a placed card one space.
Overkill
Capturing a card by at least 2x rank will lock it to your side.
